## General Settings

Navigate to **Administration → Settings** to manage:

- **Association Profile** — Name, description, logo
- **Chapter Settings** — Privacy, default membership type
- **Payment Accounts** — Bank accounts linked to chapters

## Meeting Integrations

Connect video conferencing providers for automated meeting recording and AI-powered minutes:

- **Google Meet** — OAuth connection for calendar-linked meetings
- **Zoom** — OAuth with managed token handling
- **Microsoft Teams** — OAuth connection for Teams meetings

See [Meeting Providers](/integrations/meeting-providers) for setup details.

## Membership Type Settings

Configure default behaviors for membership types including:
- Auto-recurring payment toggle
- Reminder frequency for renewals
- Workflow requirements (approval, payment)

See [Membership Types](/platform/membership-types) for details.
