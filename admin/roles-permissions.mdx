# Roles & Permissions

Transparency Hub Network uses role-based access control (RBAC) to manage what each user can see and do within an association.

## Default Roles

| Role | Members | Events | Payments | Settings | Workflows |
|------|---------|--------|----------|----------|-----------|
| **Owner** | Full | Full | Full | Full | Full |
| **Admin** | Full | Full | Full | Edit | Edit |
| **Moderator** | View/Edit | Full | View | View | View |
| **Chapter Lead** | Chapter only | Chapter only | Chapter view | — | — |
| **Member** | Self only | View/RSVP | Self only | — | — |

## Assigning Roles

<Steps>
  <Step title="Go to Members">
    Navigate to **Members** and select the member you want to update.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Change Role">
    In the member's profile, select the new role from the **Role** dropdown.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Save">
    Confirm the change. The member's permissions update immediately.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Custom Permissions

<Info>
  Custom roles are available on the **Pro** and **Enterprise** plans. Contact [support@transparencyhubnetwork.ai](mailto:support@transparencyhubnetwork.ai) for details.
</Info>

Custom roles let you define granular permissions for:
- Member management (view, create, edit, delete)
- Event management
- Payment viewing and recording
- Settings access
- Workflow configuration

## Best Practices

<Tip>
  Follow the **principle of least privilege** — assign the minimum role needed for each person's responsibilities. You can always elevate access later.
</Tip>

- Keep the number of **Owners** small (1–2 people)
- Use **Chapter Leads** to delegate regional management
- Review role assignments quarterly
