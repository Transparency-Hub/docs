## Overview

Transparency Hub Network integrates with video conferencing platforms to automatically:
- Join and record meetings via a bot ("Transparencyhub Notetaker")
- Generate transcripts
- Create AI-powered meeting minutes

## Supported Providers

| Provider | Status | OAuth Required |
|----------|--------|---------------|
| **Google Meet** | ✅ Supported | Yes |
| **Zoom** | ✅ Supported | Yes (managed by Attendee.dev) |
| **Microsoft Teams** | ✅ Supported | Yes |

## Google Meet Setup

<Steps>
  <Step title="Navigate to Settings">
    Go to **Settings → Meeting Integrations**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Connect Google Meet">
    Click **Connect** on the Google Meet card. You'll be redirected to Google's consent screen.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Authorize">
    Sign in with your Google account and grant the requested permissions.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Confirm">
    You'll be redirected back to settings with a success confirmation. Your Google email will appear on the card.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Zoom Setup

<Steps>
  <Step title="Navigate to Settings">
    Go to **Settings → Meeting Integrations**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Connect Zoom">
    Click **Connect** on the Zoom card. You'll be redirected to Zoom's authorization page.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Authorize">
    Sign in with your Zoom account and approve access.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Info>
  Zoom tokens are managed securely by our integration partner. We don't store your Zoom credentials directly.
</Info>

## Microsoft Teams Setup

Follow the same pattern as Google Meet — click **Connect**, authorize via Microsoft's consent screen, and confirm.

## How Recording Works

1. When you create an event with a meeting link and enable **Record & Generate Minutes**, a bot is automatically scheduled
2. The bot ("Transparencyhub Notetaker") joins the meeting at the scheduled time
3. It records audio/video and generates a transcript
4. AI processes the transcript into formatted meeting minutes
5. Minutes appear in the event's **Meeting Minutes** section

<Warning>
  All meeting participants will see "Transparencyhub Notetaker" join as an attendee. Ensure participants are aware of the recording.
</Warning>

## Disconnecting a Provider

Go to **Settings → Meeting Integrations**, click the menu on the provider card, and select **Disconnect**. This revokes access and removes stored credentials.
